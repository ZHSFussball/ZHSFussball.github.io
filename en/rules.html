Rules for "Football Small Field Tournament" at ZHS Freising

  1. (The rules, implementation regulations, statutes, and ordinances of the German Football Association (DFB) for small field tournaments apply.)
  2. A football team consists of a minimum of 6 and a maximum of 8 players.
  3. The game is played with 1 goalkeeper and 5 field players.
  4. The playing time is 1 x 12 minutes (without changing sides).
    Changes may occur depending on the number of participating/attending teams and the format.
  5. If there are problems with the jersey color, the team listed first on the schedule must change their jersey (or wear bibs).
    Bibs will be provided by the tournament management. The respective referee decides on this matter.
  6. "Flying substitution" is only allowed from a side line in one's own half of the field during the game.
    Exception: The goalkeeper can only be substituted during a game interruption.
  7. Wearing football shoes with studs is mandatory, wearing shin guards is strongly recommended.
  8. It is pointed out that wearing any jewelry (necklaces, earrings, piercings, etc.) is not allowed.
  9. The goalkeeper is not allowed to cross the halfway line during their team's attack.
  10. Goal kicks after the ball goes out of bounds must be played along the ground.
    The goal kick must not directly cross the halfway line. In case of an offense, an indirect free kick is awarded to the opponent at the point where the ball crossed the halfway line.
  11. If the goalkeeper takes the ball out of play, the drop kick or throw by the goalkeeper can directly cross the halfway line.
  12. The back-pass rule is valid in this tournament.
  13. The offside rule is lifted.
  14. All free kicks are indirect.
  15. Balls are thrown in from the sidelines.
  16. Penalties are taken from the 9-meter spot.
  17. 5 accumulated fouls in a match result in a penalty kick for the opposing team.
  18. If the final matches end in a draw after the regular playing time, an immediate 9-meter shootout takes place.
  19. Non-compliance: The tournament management is authorized to exclude participants from the event and remove them from the sports facility.
    The consumption of alcohol and drugs during the tournament is strictly prohibited. Players can be excluded by the tournament management for non-compliance.
